Pfannkuchen

Description

This is a recipe to the world famous German pancakes Pfannkuchen of my mother!

Ingredients

  • 250g flour
  • 500ml milk
  • 2-3 eggs
  • some sugar

Steps

  1. just throw all the ingredients together
  2. heat up a pan and put the pancakes in
  3. turn em so they don't burn
  4. enjoy
