Oat Banana Pancakes

Description

This is a recipe for Oat Bananana Pancakes I found on TikTok!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Banana
  • 1 egg
  • baking soda
  • vanilla sugar

Steps

  1. just throw all the ingredients together
  2. heat up a pan and put about 4-5 pancakes in
  3. turn em so they don't burn
  4. enjoy and eat em
